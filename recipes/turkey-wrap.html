TURKEY WRAP

DESCRIPTION

If you want to be the biggest hit at your next picnic lunch with friends, then you're in the right place. This turkey wrap recipe is mind-blowingly delicious and will have everyone begging for seconds. Keep reading if you're up to the task of being a picnic legend.

INGREDIENTS

  • Cheddar cheese
  • Turkey deli meat
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Avocado
  • Banana pepper
  • Condiment of choice
  • Tortilla wraps

STEPS

  1. Drizzle your condiment on the tortilla
  2. Add lettuce
  3. Add slice of cheese
  4. Add slices of tomato
  5. Add slices of banana pepper
  6. Add slices of avocado
  7. Add turkey meat
  8. Roll up and enjoy with your favori bag of chips
