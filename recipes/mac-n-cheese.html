MAC & CHEESE

DESCRIPTION

If you want to be the biggest hit at your next friendsgiving, then you're in the right place. This macaroni and cheese recipe is mind-blowingly delicious and will have everyone begging for seconds. Keep reading if you're up to the task of being a potluck legend.

INGREDIENTS

  • sharp cheddar cheese
  • velveeta cheese
  • heavy cream
  • elbow pasta
  • eggs
  • paprika
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • breadcrumbs

STEPS

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Butter a 9x9 inch baking dish
  3. Cook pasta until al dente, set aside when done
  4. Heat heavy cream over high until it starts to boil
  5. Add sharp cheddar and velveeta cheese to heavy cream
  6. Turn heat down to medium
  7. Stir mixture regularly until cheese is completelt melted
  8. Combine drained pasta and cheese in bowl until fully coated
  9. Add salt and pepper to taste
  10. Pour mixture into prepared pan
  11. Sprinkle leftover cheese on top
  12. Spinkle brreadcrumbs and paprika on top
  13. Put into the oven for 25 minutes or until golden brown
  14. Let cool for 15 minutes
