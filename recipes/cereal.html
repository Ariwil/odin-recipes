CEREAL

DESCRIPTION

If you want to be the biggest hit at your next breakfast gathering, then you're in the right place. This cereal recipe is mind-blowingly delicious and will have everyone begging for seconds. Keep reading if you're up to the task of being a breakfast legend.

INGREDIENTS

  • milk of your choosing
  • cereal of your choosing

STEPS

  1. Pour cereal into a cute bowl
  2. Add milk until you just see the milk under the top layer of cereal
  3. Eat with a gold spoon
